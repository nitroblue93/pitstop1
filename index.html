FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to provide water or power?+

Nope — our rig is fully self-contained with onboard water, a generator, and a hot-water system. We just need a flat spot to park and we're good to go.

How long does a detail take?+

An "In and Out" runs 2–3 hours. A "Complete Detail" is 4–5 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating are usually a full day or two — we'll give you an exact window when you book.

What if it rains?+

If you've got a covered driveway or garage, we keep going. Otherwise, we'll text you the night before and reschedule for free. No drama.

Can you do condo parking lots?+

Yes — most of our work happens in condo lots and underground garages. We just ask that you clear it with property management first.

Do you do EVs and Teslas?+

All day. EV-safe products, careful around sensors and door handles, and ceramic packages built for the soft factory paint Teslas come with.
